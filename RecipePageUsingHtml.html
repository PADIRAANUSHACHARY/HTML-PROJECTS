Simple Sponge Cake

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 100g castor sugar
  • 85g self-raising flour

Notes on ingredients

Castor sugar
Finely granulated white sugar.
Self-raising flour
A pre-mixed combination of flour and leavening agents(usually salt and baking powder).

Steps to Make Sponge Cake:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F). Grease and line a round cake tin (about 7 inches/18 cm).
  2. Beat the eggs and sugar together in a large bowl using an electric mixer. Whisk for 5-7 minutes until the mixture becomes pale, thick, and fluffy, and leaves a trail when the whisk is lifted.
  3. Sift the flour into the egg mixture gently. Fold it in using a spatula or large metal spoon. Be careful not to knock out the air from the batter.
  4. Pour the batter into the prepared cake tin, spreading it evenly.
  5. Bake for 20-25 minutes until the sponge is golden and springs back when lightly pressed.
  6. Cool in the tin for a few minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack to cool completely.
